As of March 22, 2026, St. Alexis Orthodox Church holds all services in its own building. The street address for our building is:

2323 N STATE ST UNIT 112, BUNNELL FL 32110-4398

Be sure to include "Unit 112" when entering the address in your GPS or mapping application!

N State Street is also knwon as US Hwy 1, or US-1.

The church building is on the west side of US-1 (aka N State Street) in the Atlantis Industrial Business Park.

The entrance doors are on the east side of the building, the side facing the highway.

For regularly scheduled services, please enter one of the doors marked "Entrance", near the middle of the building.

For appointments with Fr. David during the week, please enter the door marked "Office", near the southeast corner of the building.

Below is a map of the buildings within the Atlantis Industrial Business Park.

From US-1 Traveling South

NOTE: Heading south on US-1, you will turn right into the Atlantis Industrial Business Park shortly after you go through the light at the intersection of Royal Palms Pkwy/Education Way and US-1.

- Shortly after the intersection you will pass an RV park on the right.

- Get ready to turn right.

- Turn right into the Atlantis complex (North Entrance).

- Drive straight back to the 3rd row of buildings and turn left in front of that row. St. Alexis Church is a free-standing building in that row on your right.

- [Landmark: Bethel Baptist Church (a Haitian congregation) owns a small section on the north end of our building - they have a big sign on the building.]

From US-1 Traveling North

NOTE: Heading north on US-1, you will turn left into the Atlantis Industrial Business Park just under 2 miles north of Bunnell.

- Get in the left lane past the intersection where FL Hwy 100 westbound peels off of US-1 (on the right).

- You will pass the sign for Grand Reserve (on the right).

- Watch for the dedicated left turn lane and get in it. (It is directly across from The Rock on your right.)

- Turn left into the Atlantis complex, (South Entrance).

- Drive toward the storage unit business and bear right to drive in front of it and then immediately turn left.

- Just past the first row of buildings on your right, turn right. St. Alexis Church is a free-standing building in the row on your left.

- [Landmark: Bethel Baptist Church (a Haitian congregation) owns a small section on the north end of our building - they have a big sign on the building.]
