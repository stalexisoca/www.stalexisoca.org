Directions

We meet at St. Stephen Catholic Church on Sunday mornings. The street address is 2400 East Moody Boulevard (FL Highway 100), Bunnell, Florida 32110. We are on the north side of the highway, and generally located east of the Flagler County Government complex and west of Belle Terre Parkway.

From US-1 Traveling South

After entering the City of Bunnell, go past the ramp for Highway 100 West and continue south. Take a left at the light for East Moody Boulevard (FL Highway 100 East). Continue east for approximately 1.3 miles, and make a U-turn to travel west on Highway 100 for approximately one-tenth of a mile. The Church will be on your right. The main is sign for St. Stephens Catholic Church, with our smaller sign below.

From I-95 traveling south
Exit 284 and turn right onto Hwy 100. Proceed 3 1/2 miles west on Hwy 100. The church is on the right. There is a sign for St Stephens Catholic Church.

From I-95 traveling north
Exit 284 and turn left onto Hwy 100. Proceed 3 1/2 miles west on Hwy 100. The church is on the right. There is a sign for St Stephens Catholic Church.
