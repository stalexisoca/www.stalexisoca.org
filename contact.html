    Schedule of Services

    Sunday Morning

    • 9:10 AM: Hours (prayers in preparation for service)
    • 9:30 AM: Divine Liturgy (the worship service)
    • After the service all are invited to join us for Coffee and Food in the parish hall!
    • "Essentials of the Orthodox Faith" usually meets toward the end of eating and is an informal teaching and discussion open to all Catechumens, guests, and those seeking for more information about the Orthodox Church, facilitated by our Priest, Choir Director, or Catechist

    Wednesday

    • 6:00 PM: Daily prayers
    • 6:30 PM: Catechism for current Catechumens and members of the Parish

    Saturday

    • 5:00 PM: Great Vespers
    • Confession offered after service and by appointment with the Priest for our Members
